Mustafa Al Quraishi is a designer and engineer operating in London. A Design Engineering student at Imperial College London with a rooted interest in bringing ideas into fruition. Mustafa is interested in the various stages of product, systems and brand development; utilising an amalgamation of engineering, computing and design to allow himself to think freely and mix various disciplines to produce creative solutions and develop captivating brands.

Coming off national recognition at one of the largest design competition for students in the country, coming in second place at the Triumph Design awards in 2019, and a 1:1 degree score after his first year at university, Mustafa's desire and curiosity for design and technology is the driving force behind his work.
