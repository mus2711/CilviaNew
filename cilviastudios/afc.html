A FURNITURE COLLECTION

This collection is an exploration and challenge of furniture pieces. With a specific focus on the composition of geometries and materials to compliment and juxtapose each other, this collection consists of a stripped back minimalist look akin to a modernist era piece. The collection celebrates the use of metal within furniture, as rough anodised aluminium is found throughout each piece.

Design Development

This project devolved from a desire to try my hand at creating a piece of furniture. The nature of this stool consists of a stripped back minimalist look akin to a brutalist/modernist era piece. Composed entirely of an aluminium, the stool would be cold to the touch, and provide little cushioning to the user. Challenging our expectations of furniture in general, are we designing something for someone to sit on? Or to just admire the thought of sitting on it?

The chair angle and height is adjusted by the use of twist lock dials indicated by the textured grips located beneath the seat and at the mid point of the frame.

CILVIA STUDIOS IS HEADED BY CREATOR MUSTAFA AL QURAISHI, A DESIGN ENGINEER FROM IMPERIAL COLLEGE LONDON.
